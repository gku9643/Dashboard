Our Strategic Roadmap

2025 Q1-Q2: Production Setup & Initial Trials

**Manufacturing Plan:** Finalize lease and fit-out of 500-1000 sq. ft. assembly area. Procure initial machinery (CNC, welding stations). Secure initial raw material supply for 25-50 units.
Secure initial defence pilot contracts for Striker Series (ISR/Reconnaissance variant). Conduct extensive field trials with military units to gather crucial performance data.

2025 Q3-Q4: First Deliveries & Strike Variant R&D

**Production Plan:** Deliver first batch of 25-50 operational Striker units. Scale production capacity to 10-15 units per month.
Initiate advanced R&D for the **Striker-Kinetic variant**, focusing on warhead integration, terminal guidance, and safety mechanisms. Begin development of specialized test equipment for kinetic payloads.

2026 Q1-Q2: Scaled Kinetic Production & Swarm Development

**Production Plan:** Expand manufacturing capacity by 200% to support kinetic variant production, targeting 30-40 units per month. Begin mass production of both ISR and Kinetic variants.
Introduce **Striker-Kinetic variant** with precision warhead integration. Initiate R&D for multi-drone swarm control algorithms, aiming for coordinated operations of up to 5-10 loitering munitions by a single operator.

2026 Q3-Q4: Swarm Testing & International Outreach

Conduct initial tests of multi-drone swarm capabilities in controlled environments. Refine AI algorithms for autonomous collaborative missions. Begin preliminary discussions for international market penetration in allied nations, assessing regulatory pathways and market interest.

2027: Next-Gen Payloads & Global Expansion

Develop and test advanced Electronic Warfare (EW) and Communications Relay payloads for the Striker platform, significantly expanding mission versatility. Forge strategic partnerships for full international market entry. Integrate Aerax systems with larger military Command and Control (C2) systems for seamless interoperability.
